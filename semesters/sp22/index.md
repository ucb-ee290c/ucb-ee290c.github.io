## Two Chips:  BearlyML & SCUM-V

Approximately 40 students worked in two teams to design two separate chips in a 16 nm Intel process.  One was a machine learning accelerator, called BearlyML, and the other was a radio mote, called SCUM-V.  You can find the two spec sheets created at the end of the semester here:

[Datasheets - Two Taped-out Chips](https://drive.google.com/drive/u/0/folders/1DfthvPE4hBrMHG7zfgKHkvlHBVyf55A8)

The first agenda item for the follow-on Fall 2023 Bringup Class will be to read these datasheets and see if they are complete enough for the purpose of designing test boards.  [Haha!  Not even close - ksjp, 9/23]  

If not, the Fall 2023 Bringup students will contact the Spring 2022 Tapeout students and polish the docs until they're usable.
